Privacy Policy

Pinball Space Estimator

Last Updated: January 31, 2026

Summary: Pinball Space Estimator does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Your privacy is fully protected.

Data We Don't Collect

We believe in complete transparency. Here's what we explicitly do NOT collect:

  • No account creation or login required
  • No personal information (name, email, phone)
  • No location data or GPS tracking
  • No analytics or usage tracking
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No third-party SDKs or trackers

Camera Access

The app requires camera access for one purpose only:

  • LiDAR room scanning to measure your space
  • AR visualization to show pinball machine placements

All camera data is processed entirely on your device. No images or scan data are ever uploaded, stored, or transmitted anywhere.

Photo Library Access

If you choose to save AR screenshots:

  • Screenshots are saved directly to your Photos app
  • We do not access, upload, or analyze these images
  • You have complete control over these files

Local Storage

The app stores minimal data locally on your device:

  • Your onboarding completion status
  • Your preferred placement mode setting

This data never leaves your device and can be deleted by uninstalling the app.

Third-Party Services

We use zero third-party services:

  • No analytics platforms (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
  • No crash reporting services
  • No advertising networks
  • No social media integrations

Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect any data from anyone, including children. The app is rated 4+ and is safe for users of all ages.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us:
