Frequently Asked Questions

Which iPhones are supported?

PinFit requires an iPhone with LiDAR scanner: iPhone 12 Pro, iPhone 12 Pro Max, iPhone 13 Pro, iPhone 13 Pro Max, iPhone 14 Pro, iPhone 14 Pro Max, iPhone 15 Pro, or iPhone 15 Pro Max. iPad Pro models with LiDAR are also supported.

Can I use it without LiDAR?

Yes! You can manually enter your room dimensions if your device doesn't have a LiDAR scanner. You just won't be able to use the AR visualization feature.

What machine sizes are supported?

We support three standard sizes: Standard (28.5" √ó 52"), Widebody (27" √ó 56"), and Compact (22" √ó 42"). These cover the vast majority of pinball machines ever made.

How accurate is the measurement?

LiDAR scanning is highly accurate, typically within 1-2% of actual measurements. The app uses Apple's RoomPlan framework which is specifically designed for precise spatial scanning.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. All scanning and processing happens entirely on your device. We don't collect, store, or transmit any data. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
